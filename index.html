About the Firm

Built for the most demanding disputes.

Koning Zollar LLP was founded on a simple premise: clients deserve both the institutional knowledge of a large firm and the dedication and responsiveness of a boutique. Our partners bring decades of experience defending the world's leading companies in their most consequential legal matters.

We are selective about the work we take on — because when we commit to a client, we commit fully. No handoffs to junior associates. No bureaucracy. Just exceptional lawyering from people who have done this at the highest level.
